  • 978-4-06-519981-7

Incorrect example

  • 999-4-06-519981-0 (GS1 symbol must be either 978 or 979)
  • 978-4-06-519981-0 (Check digit is different)
  • 978--4-06-519981-7 (Hyphens are continuous)
  • 97840651998170 (too many digits)
  • hogehoge (everything is useless)
